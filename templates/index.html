Known Limitations

Prototype parser coverage

The current prototype supports representative text, log, CSV, JSON, and XML-style inputs. Additional source formats may require parser updates.

External enrichment

The report identifies suspicious values from uploaded logs but does not verify them against live threat intelligence feeds.
